About Hackademic

Hackademic is a cyber-education platform that teaches school kids key digital skills through gamified learning. Our mission is to make online safety and basic coding accessible, enjoyable, and practical.

What we offer

  • Interactive games to practice phishing detection, password hygiene, and core computing skills
  • Weekly leaderboards and challenges
  • Teacher-friendly progress tracking and classroom modes (coming soon)
