Phishing Detection — Hard

Core idea: Analyze emails and decide whether they are phishing or legitimate. Learn to spot common signs and patterns used by attackers.

Difficulty

Hard — realistic simulated inboxes and more sophisticated phishing samples.

Scoring

Points for correct classification; penalties for false positives/negatives. Accuracy and speed both influence the final score.

What you'll learn

  • Recognize phishing indicators in sender, subject, and body
  • Understand trade-offs: false positives vs false negatives
  • Apply careful verification before clicking links or sharing info
